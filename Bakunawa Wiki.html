Extended Definition

Bakunawa is a juvenile Dusk Leviathan, a creature of massive, untamable, and overwhelming force of chaos or power. It is the offspring of Oroboros, the Aeon of Voracity. It is not part of the Void Song Whale species, but a sibling of it.

Description

Bakunawa appears as a huge serpent-like dragon creature, its scales gradient in the color of teal and turquoise, with gold eyes and slit pupils. It has a mouth capable of swallowing stars, planets and nebulas, each crushed by its sharp teeth. It initially was born the size of an asteroid but gradually grew to the size of a planet.

Driven by inherited voracity, Bakunawa roams the void and devours any celestial body in its path — star, planet, or nebula — swallowing each whole and growing larger with every feeding. Above all, it is fixated on moons; it will abandon richer prey to chase one down, lunging with frenzied speed and plunging the surrounding space into a sudden, eclipse-like dark as it feeds.

As it drifts through the void, Bakunawa emits a low, resonant hum, felt more than heard. When it feeds, the hum sharpens into a piercing, bell-like wail over the crackle of its scales and the grinding crunch of worlds breaking apart between its teeth.

Lore & Etymology

Bakunawa is an offspring of Oroboros; it preferred to savor the stars around the empty space left behind by destroyed planets Oroboros devoured. But after the disappearance of the Aeon of Voracity (Oroboros), like its parent's creations, the Void Song Whales, Bakunawa began to devour without discrimination and developed its obsession with eating moons. It is said Bakunawa's fascination with the moons started when it accidentally ate one, mistaking it for a shooting star. The name itself derives from the Bakunawa of Philippine (Visayan) mythology — a moon-devouring sea serpent blamed for causing eclipses.

Unlocking the Unit

As the Astral Express continues its journey, it is halted by a horde of Void Song Whales intercepting the star rails, triggering the Trailblaze Mission "Hors d'oeuvre: Upon the Debris of Hubris." Unlike standard playable characters, Bakunawa cannot be pulled from Warp banners — it must be unlocked as an endgame challenge node through the following steps:

  1. Complete the prerequisite mission. Finish the Trailblaze Mission "Aperitif: Taste the Swarm" to trigger the follow-up star rail crisis.
  2. Clear the interception quest. Defeat every combat encounter in "Hors d'oeuvre: Upon the Debris of Hubris" to disperse the Void Song Whales blocking the Express.
  3. Locate the domain challenge. Open your Interastral Guide, navigate to the Echo of War tab, and teleport to "Echo of War: The First Course of Old" to begin the weekly boss fight.

Rewards

Defeating Bakunawa and clearing "Echo of War: The First Course of Old" grants the following rewards:

  • Trace Material — Saliva of a Dusk Leviathan
  • Relics and Credits
  • 4★ Light Cones
  • Lucent Afterglow
  • First-clear bonus: the Trailblaze Fashion outfit "Leviathan Headband"

Combat Behavior Pattern

Bakunawa shifts through two distinct combat forms, wielding mixed Quantum and Physical attacks while punishing teams that lean on its protected elements.

Damage Output: Quantum Physical True

Weaknesses: Wind Fire Ice

Core Passive Mechanics

Serpent's Glare Debuff

Applied automatically at the start of battle. This global aura permanently lowers your party's ATK and DEF. It also grants the Underside of the Moon buff to the boss's summoned fragments, doubling their Physical and Quantum damage.

Salt & Pepper Buff

For every Wind, Fire, or Ice character on your team, Bakunawa gains +15% DMG dealt, -15% DMG taken, and its Toughness bar becomes immune to reduction. Persists until the boss's shield is fully depleted.

Tip: Although Wind, Fire, and Ice break Bakunawa's Toughness fastest, every Wind, Fire, or Ice unit on your team also feeds Salt & Pepper. Balance your weakness-breakers against the buff they grant — or burst its shield quickly to strip the protection before it snowballs.

Phase 1: The Offspring of Voracity

Serpent's Tears Talent

Summons 4 Juvenile Black Holes. Damage dealt to them increases the damage of the upcoming Knife for Savory. Afterward, any remaining Juvenile Black Holes morph into Limbs of the Devourer minions.

Serpent's Fork Single Target Physical 300% ATK

Deals Physical DMG to a single target.

Knife for Savory AoE Quantum 400-500% ATK

Deals Quantum DMG to all targets.

A Spoonful of the Cosmos Ultimate 500% ATK True

If any Serpent's Tears remain, Bakunawa devours them, gaining a stacking Quantum and Physical DMG buff based on the number eaten, then deals True DMG to the entire party.

Phase 2: The Moon-Devourer

Hunger of a Leviathan Phase Transition

Triggered once all Limbs of the Devourer are eliminated. Bakunawa evolves into "The Moon-Devourer," raising its Physical and Quantum output to 450% and draining 5% of each party member's Max HP every round.

Remnants of the Moon Single Target Quantum 450% ATK

Fires a precise beam at the highest-threat character.

Fangs of Lunar Taste AoE Physical 600% ATK

Rears back and strikes the entire field with massive Physical DMG.

Moon Devourer of New Enraged Ultimate 700% ATK True

After 5 turns, Bakunawa absorbs all incoming damage, then unleashes True DMG to all targets and enters Serpent's Hunger — a permanent x3 multiplier to its Quantum and Physical attacks.
